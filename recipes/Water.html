Crispy Water

Description:

Crispy water is a new water recipe that helps you reimagine old lame bland water, into a crispy crunchy h20 refreshing drink!

Ingredients:

  • Ice
  • Frozen water

Steps:

  1. Mix the ice and frozen water
  2. Put mixture in a pan and heat it
  3. Put in fridge, wait 15 mintues and enjoy!
